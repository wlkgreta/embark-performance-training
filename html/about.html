At Embark Performance Training, we strive to provide our clients with a safe haven of health, strength, and support. We use a holistic approach of scientific support and real world practices to curate the best individualized training programs and address our clients' goals in a responsible, healthy, and sustainable way.

Matthew Barker, M.S., CSCS

Coach Barker graduated from Central College with a Bachelor's in Exercise Science and earned his Master's degree in Human Performance from Lindenwood University, where he also served as the Head Assistant Weightlifting Coach. He is a certified strength and conditioning specialist (CSCS) through the NSCA and a certified National Coach for USAW. Professionally, he has worked at the collegiate level in all divisions as a strength and conditioning coach; he has also served as a professor of Kinesiology at both Simpson College and Central College. He has worked with clients with varying skill levels, from the general population to elite athletes at the collegiate, professional, and Olympic level. Embark Performance Training is based in Des Moines, IA.
